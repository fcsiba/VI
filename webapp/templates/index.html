Speech Recognition

Our speech recognition engine enables you to understand your customers in their natural and native language. Your customers no longer have to interact with complex and boring interfaces. With our services customers can just speak out their queries, concerns, and complaints without hassle and the assurance that you will be listening.

Sentiment Analysis

Is your customer happy or upset? We're sure they want you to know their emotions about your product/service. A happy customer must always come back to you, and an angry one must never leave. Voice Intelligence is the tool you need to understand your customer's emotions so you can serve them better. This is your first step to changing negativity about your product into positivity for making better business.

Subject Categorization

What is it that concerns your customer the most? Are they curious about a product or are they frustrated with a service? You surely want to know which of your products or services are your customers talking about the most. Our Urdu subject categorization feature will help you identify major concern areas of your customers so that you know which products are on their way to becoming highly popular and which of them need more attention.

Visualize Information

Our detailed insights provide you with the capacity to gain an accurate and deep understanding of what your customers are reaching out to tell you. With detailed insights like which products are receiving the most positive customer feedback and what your customers are complaining about, you will be able to respond to your customer's needs in real time.
